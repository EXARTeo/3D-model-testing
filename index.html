Our Story

A Home Away From Home Since 1892

Nestled in the heart of Nafplio's old town, Thalassa Stone Rooms occupies a beautifully preserved neoclassical building just steps from Arvanitia Beach. Our family has welcomed travelers for three generations, offering an authentic taste of Greek life.

Each room tells a story through hand-selected antiques, local textiles, and views that have inspired poets and painters for centuries. Here, modern comfort meets timeless elegance.
